Pumpkin Waffles

Description

Fluffy, spiced waffles infused with pumpkin and warm autumn flavors. Perfect for cozy mornings, topped with syrup, whipped cream, or a sprinkle of cinnamon for a delicious fall breakfast treat!

Ingredients

  • 1 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 cup pumpkin purée
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ cup milk
  • ¼ melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat: heat waffle iron.
  2. Mix: whisk dry ingredients in one bowl, wet ingredients in another. Combine until smooth.
  3. Cook: pour batter into waffle iron and cook according to your waffle maker’s instructions until golden brown.
  4. Serve: top with syrup, whipped cream, or cinnamon sugar and enjoy!
