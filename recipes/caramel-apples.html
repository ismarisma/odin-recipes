Caramel Apples

Home

Description

Crisp, juicy apples dipped in rich, buttery caramel and coated with your favorite toppings. A classic fall treat that's sweet, sticky, and irresistibly fun to make and eat!

Ingredients

  • 6 medium apples (Granny Smith or Honeycrisp)
  • 14 oz bag soft caramels
  • 2 tbsp heavy cream
  • Wooden sticks or skewers
  • Sprinkles

Steps

  1. Prep: Wash and dry apples well, then insert sticks.
  2. Melt: Heat caramels and cream in a saucepan, stirring until smooth.
  3. Dip: Swirl each apple in caramel, letting excess drip off.
  4. Set: Place on parchment paper and chill until firm.
  5. Decorate: Drizzle with sprinkles.
